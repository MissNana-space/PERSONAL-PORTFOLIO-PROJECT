About Me

I am a Psychology Student with a strong passion for understanding human behaviour. Over the course of my academic journey, I have actively pursued opportunities to contribute to the mental health sector. Currently, I am part of the research and education team at the Coalition for the Advancement of Nigerian Mental Health (CANMH) , where I collaborate with my supervisor to design and implement mentorship programs for students pursuing psychology and mental health careers.

Outside of my academic work, I write under the pen name "ECHOES OF HER MIND." Through my writing, I express my thoughts and feelings about life and human behaviour. As I continue my studies, I hope to become a leading voice in raising mental health awareness and advocating for positive change

Projects

Perceived Emotional Neglect as a Predictor of Marital Satisfaction and Psychological Distress Among Married Women in Osun State

This is my ongoing final year research project,which looks at how perceived spousal neglect can affect the mental wellbeing and quality of marital relationships. In addition,it investiages the influence of their emotion regulation skills. The aim is to better understand the challenges faced by women in marriage and to help improve their marital Satisfaction and mental health
