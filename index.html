About Me

I am a Psychology Student with a strong passion for understanding human behaviour. Over the course of my academic journey, I have actively pursued opportunities to contribute to the mental health sector. I have volunteered with platforms such as the 7 Cups of Tea, A Minute With a Psychologist and the Coalition for the Advancement of Nigerian Mental Health (CANMH).

Outside of my academic work, I write under the pen name "ECHOES OF HER MIND." Through my writing, I express my thoughts and feelings about life and human behaviour. As I continue my studies, I hope to become a leading voice in raising mental health awareness and advocating for positive change

Projects

Final Year Research Project

My final year research project focused on how perceived spousal emotional neglect can affect the mental wellbeing and quality of marital relationships of married women living in Osun State. In addition,it investiages the impact of their emotion regulation skills in these relationships. The aim is to better understand the challenges faced by women in marriage and to help improve their marital satisfaction and mental health

Psychology Mentorship Program

Currently, I am part of the research and education team at the Coalition for the Advancement of Nigerian Mental Health (CANMH), where I collaborate with my supervisor to design and implement mentorship programs for students pursuing psychology and mental health careers.
